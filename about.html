about

Born to Chinese-Taiwanese immigrants, Kelley Tai (she/her) is a speculative fiction writer and a 2024 CSFFA Aurora Award-nominated poet. When she's not writing, you can find her cuddling with her two kitties and obsessively testing travel bags in pursuit of the ultimate carry-on companion.

For any business inquires, you can email Kelley at kelleyandbramble at gmail dot com.
