about

Kelley Tai is an Aurora Award-nominated science fiction and fantasy writer sometimes based in Canada, sometimes in the United States. She is the Founding Editor of Bramble & Crow Books and the Managing Editor, Online at Augur Society. In her free time, Kelley enjoys cuddling with her two kitties, playing around with mechanical keyboards, and traveling to unforgettable destinations.

For any business inquires, you can email Kelley at kelleyandbramble at gmail dot com.
