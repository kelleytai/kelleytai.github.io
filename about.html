about

Kelley Tai (she/her) is a science fiction and fantasy writer who daydreams about alien plants and wishes she were part-cat.

She runs her own freelance editing business at Bramble and Crow Books and is the Online Editor for Augur Magazine.

When she's not busy herding her two kitties, Kelley occasionally makes videos on YouTube about the books she's writing and reading.

For any business inquires, you can email her at kelleyandbramble at gmail dot com.
