About me

Hello World!

I am a third-year student at the University of Toronto studying computer science and linguistics. Although my main focus is computational linguistics, the computer processing, understanding, and generation of human languages, I am also interested in programming on the web and UX/UI design. I like to make things pretty.

As for linguistics, I throughouly enjoy first-language acqusition and sociolinguistics. If you are a random stranger talking to me, I will be analyzing your speech so try not to worry when I ask you where you are from. It's all in the name of science.

Random Facts About Yours Truly

I like to take pictures of things. In my free time, I also like to battle you. Most importantly, I like to read books. And sometimes I like to write them too.
