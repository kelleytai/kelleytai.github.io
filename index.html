kelley tai

about

Born to Chinese-Taiwanese immigrants, Kelley Tai is a Canadian-American speculative fiction writer and a 2024 CSFFA Aurora Award-nominated poet. She graduated from the University of Toronto with a degree in Computer Science and spends her day as a Product Owner for one of the largest banks in the world. She is currently based in NJ/NYC where she lives with her husband and two kitties.

Note: Kelley's editorial services at Bramble & Crow Books are on hiatus indefinitely.
