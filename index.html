My Volunteer

Future Blue Team Lead

September 2015 - August 2016

During my 16-month internship at IBM, I was a Future Blue Team Lead. Future Blue is a community for students, by students, at IBM Canada. Future Blue plans events and programs at work and after hours to help enrich the student experience. We also provide resources and assistance to help students transit into their new roles. During this time, I met a crazy amount of people. I honestly believe this has been one of the most eye-opening experience I've had in my life. I learned a crazy lot about working with people, working with myself, and most importantly, seeing the world as Opportunity (yes, with an upper-case O). All you need to do is go out of your comfort zone and grab Opportunity and everything will be okay.
