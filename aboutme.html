Bio

I love bridging the gap between being a technical person and being a creative person. I graduated from the University of Toronto with a degree in Computer Science, mostly focusing on human-computer interaction and computational linguistics. Some of the skills I bring with me anywhere include programming, user research, UX design, content design, and consulting.

There's nothing that makes me happier than working hard with a group of fun and intelligent people.

Outside of my professional environment, I am a reader, I am a writer, I am a backpacker. I sing, I lift weights, I play board games and I am always spreading environmental awareness one or another. I also make videos on YouTube.
